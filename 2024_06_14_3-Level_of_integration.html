The state of things on Earth

We now have a model of the levels of development humanity went through:

1. Total Violence - I will kill you (and take your means of production).

This is the natural state of things in nature - the Tribal system. Our Tribal system was of course more developed and more diverse but at the model level, this is it. Two groups of people met and the chance of total violence was determined by their level of familiarity and ability to communicate and find a compromise.

This stage lasted for tens of millions of years (at least, and is still there for non-humans). It is still interwoven into our lives in many societies (along with more advanced forms of existence).

Update 6/22 For the largest part of this period, humanity and its predecessors were in a physical animal state. In the later stages (arguably between 3 million and up to ~200,000 years ago) humanity was in a physical semi-animal state. Subsequently, anatomically modern humans evolved but our thinking remained deeply tied to nature, and the thinking was still stuck mostly in a naturalistic/animalistic frame of reference (event as we now know at least to the time of GÃ¶bekli Tepe), lasting to at least 10,000 years ago. Naturalistic and animalistic cultural elements and thinking/ideology are still present everywhere in the world but are much more pronounced in regions like Africa, North American reservations (and presumably in other parts of the Americas), and the Eurasian Northeast. Per the MTABS model this lasted from ~700,000 to ~700 BCE (and was augmented with later stages at ~70,000 and ~7,000 BCE).


2. Systemized Violence - I will own you (and use you as a means of production).

Of course, we know that there were slave warriors and even slave armies but for the sake of the model, the idea is that Slavery deprived individuals of their rights, forced them to work, but kept them alive in exchange (if they obediently followed orders).

This stage lasted for (arguably tens of) thousands of years and was only finally officially dismantled in the 20th century. It is still unofficially present in at least some parts of the world.

Update 6/22 Human slavery on our planet is traceable to 11,000 years ago (at least). During this period the mental matrix of the society was keeping the (increasingly larger portions of the) population in a semi-animal state, where they were owned by either a government or private parties. Per the MTABS model, this period lasted from ~70,000 years ago to (arguably and with variations between regions) ~70 years ago, with regional variation of late 19th to mid 20th century, at the end of the institutionalized practice of slavery in the USA, serfdom Russian Empire and until unyielding, total enforcement of no slavery in China by CCP, where Slavery and Feudalism overlapped for thousands of years.


3. Open Threat of Violence - I will kill you if (you don't give me your means of production).

For most of the people in it, Feudalism meant working someone else's land and keeping the excess that wasn't taken away from you (while also not being able to leave the land you worked). For the sake of the model, this also meant that you didn't have to fight when the wars were fought (leaving that to the free men) freeing you from the high chance of premature death, as long as you followed the rules imposed on you.

This stage lasted for (a) thousand(s) years (depending on where in the world you were). Quite a bit of the World unofficially continues to live by the rule of the open threat of violence, even if the economic system is no longer tied to the land.

Update 6/22 Feudalism has been in our society starting probably 7,000 years ago (since we've been growing crops and protecting the land on which we grew them, especially in the more civilized regions, likely evolved with some level of external help). The mentality of Feudalism is more clearly human but spiritually tying humans to territory kept them in a Semi-Animal Mental and even more so Spiritual state. In Europe, this was clearly defined with pre-reform Christianity and its nomenclature. This shift political fragmentation and overarching spiritual control marks the references to this (regionally widely differing) period.


4. Hidden Threat of Violence - I will fire you if (whatever).

Much of the world is now living in some version of the system (often interwoven with the previous systems). This is an unnatural stage as the presence of violence takes a hard-to-determine but repulsive form that demotivates people - most people (still) don't like their jobs (and the environments they work in). But much more importantly there is no more systematic "I will kill you if". But at least in some parts of the world workplaces are progressing to motivate people by how they change the world for the better and not by "I will fire you if".

This stage lasted for hundreds of years in some parts of the world and was largely skipped by most of the world (at least in its pure form) which had a higher proportion of tribal and feudal elements.

Update 6/22 Focus away from land-based wealth to capital-based wealth is logically the final step of mental externalization of means of production away from humans (just as Fedualism presumably was on the physical level). Due to the (gradually reducing) focus on capital building and not (yet) on the (gradually increasing, with some spiraling perturbations and regional variations) welfare of the members of society, the system is keeping us in a spiritual semi-animal state. The time period (per MTABS model) is roughly from 700 years ago to the present.


5. No Threat of Violence - We hope you join us because ...

We are now on the verge of this stage, to be brought about by nuclear fusion and robotization, which will increase productivity (eventually by an order of magnitude and more). People will still work of course, but they won't (eventually) do so to make a basic living but instead will do so to help people around them (and the greater world).

Based on the assumption that the trend inverts (like an hourglass) this stage will last for (a) thousand(s of) years.

Updates 6/22, 1/17/2025 Having lived in an anti-capitalist society that attempted to build the heavens-rebellious state of juvenile Communism, with Socialist designs (Soviet democracy) but lacking implementation due to heavy Feudal influence and the associated imperial thinking (in partial defense, it be said that it was forced on the society at least to preserve its statehood against foreign aggression, which was also the cause of excessive centralization) I know what it felt like not to be seen a "dollar sign". Thus, it can be abstracted away that starting with the upcoming early version of "Social"ism/"Human"ism we will finally start to achieve the spiritual human state, thus completing the triad of physical, mental, and spiritual humanhood. Likely the following advancements will deal with us helping other intelligent species achieve the same. Eventually these advancements will help all of the inhabitants greater Solar System achieve inter-planetary No Threat of Violence. Per MTABS model this period began to manifest itself after the end of Systemized Violence (with post WWII global social changes) and will last on Earth until ~70,000 years from now (when we conclude dry-surface inter-governmental violent conflict on Earth).


6. No Violence - No one will kill you or hurt you (in any way, guaranteed, no matter who you are or where you are). This is of course within the bounds of dry surface of our planet, which by then will no longer be politically divided.

This is the state we've all been dreaming of for a very long time but we have a long way to go before we can truly get there.

By the hourglass principle, per the MTABS model this stage will last from Phase 5 (~70,000 years from now) and through Phase 7 and possibly beyond. Though by that time it will likely be largely up to us to directly (but with respect for time) evolve the course of events and determine the vector of the advancement of our biological progress.


7. Unknown

It can only be speculated that beyond violence humanity will be faced with the ultimate question of mortals - Death. Thus a similar (in Mental and Physical plains) progression can be built for phases 8 and beyond. In either case, as far as we know Mohammad was the only person to walk in and out of heaven ...
