Many Updates: 1/30/2025 - Alive, Whole and Undamaged or "All Whales Under Hull Like Happiness & Kindness"

/ > notes


With the assumption made in the previous note, within 100 years we will realize that we are guardians of several species of intelligent beings, native to our Solar System. As our technological capabilities increase (drastically reducing in-Solar System travel times and increasing payload capacity) we will come into more regular and tight contact with the most advanced of them, potentially showing them God's light by (gradually) introducing them to Earth's oceans, starting with well controlled regions and expanding (with proven symbiotic existence) to let them eventually develop their own space faring capabilities. In our dealings with these intelligent beings, we must avoid deadly, mutilating or seriously damaging conflict and ensure that all intelligent beings have the right to be:
  • Alive,
  • Whole (not mutilated),
  • Undamaged (not seriously damaged),
  • Healthy
And the right to live:
  • Long
  • Happily
  • Kindly
Avoiding conflict with the Solar System co-located (and potentially co-located aquatically, on Earth) intelligent species will set the correct precedent for ourselves and extra-solar space-faring species. It is the morally correct thing to do and also extremely beneficial for the human species, our survival, and our independence.

We must remember that in our solar system, two additional dry-surface planets are theoretically capable of (and both may already be) hosting life and will eventually (likely with external help) be populated on their surfaces. Thus we will likely be in the same system as other space-faring species and must be prepared to take the best course of action for everyone by instilling the God-given truths in our society and culture.
