MTABS model started as a listing of accomplishments to be made by humanity to achieve the goals of eternal Sun and Earth and to contribute to greater long term Galactic goals. After a period of reflection the underlying life-cycle driver of the model provided a perspective in which humanity starts at its stellar birth and continues through growing, family and extended family building ages and beyond. Naturally (even instinctively), humanity needs to be rooted in realities of planetary life in order to successfully co-raise and eventually protect upcoming races to interstellar travel and beyond. Beyond, in it's Servant Leadership Age humanity's continued commitment to it's home planet and star will enable it to correctly guide a multitude of newer races at various stages of development. Having a physical stake in maintaining the eternal life cycle of the Sun and remaining on Earth until it is ready for another birthing cycle will ensure the correct mindset, help co-accomplish long term Galactic goals and improve life cycles of future races in the Solar System and beyond.
